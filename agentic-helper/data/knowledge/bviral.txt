bviral is a media licencer company 
Why does BVIRAL exist?
BVIRAL exists to redefine how video is valued. Viral video has long been treated as disposable—but it’s not. It’s
intellectual property with real value. BVIRAL exists to protect, distribute, and monetize that value.
What does our future look like?
We’re building a world where video is treated like the asset it truly is—protected, respected, and built to last. As the
creator economy matures, BVIRAL will be at the center of it: powering smarter rights management, broader distribution,
and bigger outcomes for the people behind the content.
How will we create that future?

We combine scalable licensing, smart distribution, and IP protection through tools like IPSHIELD to turn video into a long-
term asset. We leverage our relationships with top platforms to support leading creators, while also delivering

exceptional service to the brands and publishers who rely on their content.
Who do we serve?
We work with creators, rights holders, brands, and publishers behind the internet’s most impactful content. We also aim
to build the best creator base and video library anywhere—by serving both top performers and rising talent we believe in.
Whether they’re already leading or just getting started, we help them go further, faster.
What makes us different?
We don’t just move content—we build the systems behind it. While others focus on visibility, we focus on long-term
value, helping creators and partners get more from their videos without changing how they create.
